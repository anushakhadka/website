Who am i?

It looks really beautiful and fresh at dawn. There is no smoke and no noise. The roads are all clean. Cool breeze blows at that time. Very nice environment and the graden, the greenery makes you look at it twice. Birds starts chirping like saying hello to everyone. More Information

At dawn my locality looks ineffably ethereal and sublime, as if belonging to some other divine planet. There is soul-soothing serenity everywhere. A refreshing and revitalizing breeze is blowing until the crowds of people begin to throng streets and lanes. The sky is far more peaceful and celestial with beautiful diamond-like stars twinkling peacefully. They don’t appear so far distant. There is no deafening din of human civilization. The tensions, worries, and concerns don’t confound the human heart. There is promise of brighter future in the peace of the dawn. There is healing in the early hours of dawn. It is very unfortunate that very few people get up early to enjoy the blessings that the heavens pour down on earth during dawn.
